About Me


I'm so excited to welcome you into my world and share the journey that has shaped who I am today. My name is Nabila, and I am a passionate multitalent who thrives at the intersection of creativity and entrepreneurship, dedicated to making a positive impact through my work in the fashion industry and beyond. Currently pursuing my studies in Fashion Design and Business Management at Universitas Negeri Surabaya, I have cultivated a diverse skill set that encompasses **modeling, fashion styling, digital content creation, and social media strategy**. This multifaceted approach allows me to navigate the complexities of the fashion world while crafting innovative solutions that resonate with a broad audience. Over the years, I have been fortunate to gather a rich tapestry of experiences, ranging from internships with prominent fashion houses where I honed my design skills, to volunteering at local community events that promote sustainable fashion. Notably, my participation in many competition not only showcased my work but also solidified my commitment to empowering women through modest fashion. Each of these experiences has significantly contributed to my growth and understanding of the industry, as well as my passion for advocating social causes. What drives me is not merely the pursuit of personal success, but a deep-seated commitment to contribute meaningfully to the fashion community and the broader society. I actively engage in initiatives aimed at promoting body positivity and inclusivity in fashion, believing that every individual deserves to feel confident and beautiful in their skin. Outside of my professional life, I am an enthusiastic traveler and artist, often immersing myself in diverse cultures that inspire my creativity. Whether I’m exploring the vibrant streets of [sebutkan kota atau negara yang pernah dikunjungi], capturing moments through photography, or expressing my emotions on canvas, I believe that every experience adds depth to my identity and fuels my artistic vision. Through this portfolio, I aim not only to showcase my achievements and projects but also to connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for innovation, sustainability, and the transformative power of fashion. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me; I invite you to explore my work, engage in meaningful conversations, and join me on this exciting journey of discovery and inspiration!
